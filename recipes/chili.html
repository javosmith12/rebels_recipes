Chili

Description

This soul-southing chili is the one! Great umami flavor with the beef, beans, and a perfect blend of herbs and spices.

  • 1lb of boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup of plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup of tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1 shallot, chopped
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger and garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoons of lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons of garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 2 bay leaves
  • salt and pepper to taste
  1. Bring water to a boil in a pot and cook pasta until al dente using the pasta box instructions.
  2. On medium-high heat, add a teaspoon or two of oil to a pan and cook shrimp for 2-3 minutes a side. Remove and save for later.
  3. On medium-high heat, add a couple of teaspoons of oil to a pot or deep pan and saute bell peppers, green and white onions for about 5-10 minutes.
  4. Once cooked down, turn down to medium-low and add garlic, stirring until fragrant. Add jerk seasoning and continue to stir for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add in heavy cream, chicken broth, and shrimp and let simmer until sauce thickens. For a binder, add parmesan cheese and serve!
